### FAQ Section (Also AI Training Data)

**Q1: How long should I keep the henna on for the best stain?**
> Keep it on for 8-12 hours for a deep maroon. Minimum 6 hours for a light stain. Overnight is ideal. Apply before bed, remove in the morning.

**Q2: How do I store the cone after I receive it?**
> Straight to the freezer! These cones have no preservatives, so the freezer is what keeps them active. They'll last 3-4 months frozen. Once thawed, use within a day or two.

**Q3: What if I don't freeze it?**
> If left outside the freezer for more than 3 days, the cone will lose its potency. The stain will come out light or orange instead of deep maroon. Please freeze immediately. It makes all the difference.

**Q4: Can I order if I live outside Kerala?**
> You can, but please be aware of transit times. If delivery might take more than 3 days, the cone can spoil in transit. For locations outside Kerala, please WhatsApp us first at +91 7561856754 so we can advise on shipping options.

**Q5: Is this safe for children?**
> Yes, our henna has no PPD, no preservatives, no chemicals. It's as natural as henna gets. That said, always do a small patch test first, especially for very young children or anyone with sensitive skin.

**Q6: How long does the stain last?**
> A well-applied stain lasts **8-12 days**. It can fade faster to 3-6 days if you wash dishes frequently, swim in chlorinated water, or use strong soaps. The first 24 hours after removal are crucial. Avoid water to lock in the deepest color.

**Q7: My stain looks orange. What happened?**
> This usually means the henna wasn't kept on long enough (try 10+ hours next time) or the cone wasn't stored properly. If the cone was outside the freezer for more than 3 days before use, the dye may have degraded. Always freeze immediately!

**Q8: Can I use the skin cone for nail art?**
> You can, but the nail cone has a finer tip for more precision. The skin cone has a wider opening better suited for larger designs. We recommend the nail cone for detailed work.

**Q9: How do I order?**
> Browse our products, add to cart, then click "Order via WhatsApp." This opens a pre-filled message to Chippy with your order details. She'll confirm and arrange delivery.

**Q10: Do you do bridal henna?**
> Yes! Chippy takes bridal bookings for weddings in and around Malappuram. WhatsApp her at +91 7561856754 with your wedding date and location to check availability.

**Q11: Do you ship outside Kerala?**
> We ship across Kerala regularly. For outside Kerala, transit time is the concern. If delivery exceeds 3 days, the product may spoil. WhatsApp us to discuss express shipping options.

**Q12: What is PPD and why should I avoid it?**
> Para-phenylenediamine (PPD) is a chemical found in many commercial henna products, especially "black henna." It can cause severe skin reactions, burns, and long-term sensitization. Our henna contains zero PPD. Your safety is our non-negotiable.

**Q13: Can I apply henna on already dry/cracked skin?**
> Henna absorbs better into well-hydrated skin. We recommend moisturizing the area 30 minutes before application, then wiping dry. Avoid applying on cuts or irritated skin.

**Q14: How do I get the cone flowing if it's clogged?**
> Gently warm the tip between your fingers for 30 seconds. If the opening is blocked, use a clean pin to clear it. Never use force. It can burst the cone.

**Q15: Can pregnant women use this henna?**
> Our henna is 100% natural, but we always recommend consulting your doctor before any new product during pregnancy. Many mothers use natural henna safely, but your doctor's advice comes first.
